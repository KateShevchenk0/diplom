# Golden Gate

The Golden Gate is a famous architectural monument located in the center of Kyiv, Ukraine. This is a reconstruction of the entrance to the ancient Russian city of Kyiv, which existed in the XI-XIII centuries. The Golden Gate was restored in 1982 and became a symbol of the city.
Architecturally, the Golden Gate reflects the style of Kyivan Rus, using Byzantine and Kyivan architectural elements. They have two towers located on either side of the gate, as well as depictions of saints and historical events on the facade.
In medieval Kyiv, the Golden Gate was an important defensive structure and the entrance to the city. Today, they are a tourist attraction that attracts visitors with its historical value and beauty. The Golden Gate also became a symbol of Ukraine's independence and national self-determination.
