# Odessa Opera House

Odessa Opera House is one of the most outstanding architectural buildings and cultural monuments of Odessa, Ukraine. Founded in 1887, it is of great importance as one of the largest opera houses not only in Ukraine, but also in the world.
The building of the Odessa Opera House impresses with its elegant architecture in the Neo-Renaissance style. It is located on Teatralnaya Street, in the very heart of the historic city center. The facade of the theater is decorated with a colonnade, sculptures and statues of prominent composers.
Grandeur and luxury reign inside the theater. The hall impresses with its beauty and ceiling painting. Large chandeliers, soft chairs, sophisticated decor create a unique atmosphere. Acrobats, opera performances, ballets and concerts, which take place in the Odessa Opera House, always excite and delight the audience.
