# Castle in Kamianka

The castle in Kamianets-Podilskyi is an outstanding historical and architectural monument located in the picturesque town of Kamianets-Podilskyi, in the south-west of Ukraine. This medieval castle is one of the most impressive fortifications in Ukraine.
The castle in Kamianets-Podilskyi is located on a rock above the Smid River, which gives it a breathtaking panoramic view. It consists of a series of defensive structures, including towers, turrets, walls and palace structures. The castle was founded in the 13th century and has undergone several stages of construction and reconstruction throughout its history.
Visitors to the castle can enjoy noteworthy guided tours that allow you to learn a lot about the history of the castle and its role in important historical events. The interior of the castle impresses with its authenticity and atmosphere of the past.
