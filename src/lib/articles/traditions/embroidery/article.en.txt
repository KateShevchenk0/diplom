# Embroidery

Embroidery is an important tradition of Ukraine, which embodies beauty and art in fabric painting. Embroidery plays a major role in the cultural heritage of the Ukrainian people and is an integral part of the Ukrainian national identity.
Ukrainian embroidery is distinguished by the originality of ornaments and symbols, which convey the significance of historical events, regional features and folk beliefs. Embroidery ornaments can be geometric, floral, animal or a combination of different elements. Each embroidered pattern has its own unique symbolism and meaning.
Traditionally, vyshyvankas were used as festive clothes during solemn events, weddings, public holidays and religious ceremonies. Vyshyvanka were of great importance in popular belief and are connected with the worldview of the Ukrainian people. They embody wishes for good, health, wealth and happiness.
