# Ukrainian thought

Ukrainian dumas are majestic poetic works that have a deep historical meaning and reflect the heroism and struggle of the Ukrainian people. They are one of the most significant genres of Ukrainian epic poetry and have great value as part of Ukrainian national cultural heritage.
Ukrainian Dumas reflect historical events, battles and exploits of heroes. They tell about the liberation struggle of the Ukrainian people, about the constant struggle for freedom and independence. These epic poems have a deep sense of patriotism and national dignity.
Ukrainian Dumas are characterized by a unique style and melodiousness. They are performed as sung poems accompanied by music. Dumas are traditionally performed by bandurists who skillfully convey the depth of feelings and mood of these poems.
Ukrainian dumas have great artistic value, as they not only tell about historical events, but also convey the spirituality, willpower and national self-awareness of the Ukrainian people.
