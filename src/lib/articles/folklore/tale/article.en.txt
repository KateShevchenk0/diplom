# Ukrainian fairytale

Ukrainian fairy tales are wonderful folk tales with a rich tradition and deep symbolism. They convey ancient ideas and beliefs of the Ukrainian people, as well as teach moral values and life principles.
Ukrainian fairy tales reflect a variety of themes and characters. They tell about travel, adventure, friendship, love, the fight against evil and the victory of good. These fairy tales feature characters such as princesses, magical beasts, wolves, cats, wizards, peasants, and many others.
Ukrainian fairy tales have their own special atmosphere and style. They are connected with nature, sounds of nature, seasons and traditions of the Ukrainian village. Often in fairy tales there are magical objects, such as a blanket-hat, viburnum, clay duck and others, which give the stories a special magical mood.
