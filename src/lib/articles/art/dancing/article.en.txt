# Dancing

Ukrainian dances are an important component of Ukrainian culture and national self-determination. They reflect the richness and diversity of Ukrainian rites, traditions, folk beliefs and history. Ukrainian dances are characterized by energy, grace, complex leg movements and polyphonic group performances. Here are some outstanding Ukrainian dances:
Hopak: Hopak is the most famous Ukrainian dance. It is a fun and energetic dance performed with great mobility and acrobatic elements. Hopak is often performed solo, but can also be performed in group productions.
Polonyna: Polonyna is a traditional Ukrainian dance that reflects the image of a mountain pasture. It is characterized by smooth movements, raised hands and graceful turns. This dance is performed both solo and in a group.
