# Folk crafts

Ukraine is famous for its variety of folk crafts, which are passed down from generation to generation. These crafts embody centuries-old traditions, the uniqueness of folk art and the skill of Ukrainian craftsmen.
Ukrainian folk crafts cover a wide range of diverse industries, including textile arts, embroidery, pottery, wood carving, blacksmithing, carpets, and many others. Each of these crafts has its own unique technique, style and symbolism, which reflect the history, culture and national nature of Ukraine.
One of the most popular folk crafts of Ukraine is embroidery. Vyshyvanka, traditional Ukrainian shirts with embroidery, are a symbol of the national image. Each region has its own embroidery patterns that convey the peculiarities of local culture and customs.
